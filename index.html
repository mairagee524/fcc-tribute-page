Biography

Selena was born in Lake Jackson, Texas on April 16, 1971. She started her music career when she was a young girl, singing in front of customers at her dad's restaurant.

As she got older, Selena and her siblings (brother Abraham and sister Suzette) created a band that played various genres, from cumbia to nortenos. The band was named "Selena y Los Dinos".

Selena was about to cross-over successfully in the English language with her first English album, when she was murdered by the president of her fan club, Yolanda Saldivar. This took place in March 31st, 1995 in a motel, located in Corpus Christi, Texas.

Today, her memory carries on as people still play her music. Many fan pay tribute to her by wearing her outfits and singing her songs in public. She is such an icon to every Mexican-American.
